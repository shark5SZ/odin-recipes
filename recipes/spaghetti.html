Spaghetti

Description

This is a beautifully prepared spaghetti with love from Nigeria.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 pounds fresh tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic powder; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add tomatoes, sugar, basil, parsley, and salt; bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until sauce thickens, 1 to 2 hours.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
